Scholarship Room

Located on the 4th floor, the Scholarship Room is our most popular space, with two-story ceilings and a mezzanine overlooking the room. The wall of windows looking to the west offer panoramic views of the Salt Lake Valley and Oquirrh Mountains while windows to the east show off the stadium bowl and field as well as the beautiful Wasatch Mountains. This space can accommodate up to 900 guests and comes as a blank slate for you to customize to your exact specifications.
